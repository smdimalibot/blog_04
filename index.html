How Is Artificial Intelligence Affecting Our Daily Life?


Article Link

Published Date: October 6th, 2023

This article discusses about how Artifical Intelligence (AI) is affecting our daily lives. Artifical Intelligence can impact our lives in multiple ways such as emails (i.e., Gmail, Outlook), mobile apps (i.e., Google Properties), social networking (i.e., Instagram, Facebook), online shopping (i.e., Amazon, eBay) Mobile Banking (i.e., CIBC, Scotiabank), equipping personal assistants (i.e., Siri, Alexa and Google) and even involved in travel apps (i.e., Google Maps). Whether good or not, AI has become a widespread trend that can occur every technological field, especially today in the modern era. Therefore, AI is almost unavoidable and impacts the modern life.
